Vita Nova
Brand Design, Webflow Development,
Custom Website Design

Vita Nova was founded by Asher Schmitz (left) and Josh Bundren (right) in San Diego, California. Having a desire to start some sort of brand, they both came up with the idea of Vita Nova (New Life in Latin) during the summer of 2021. They created a brand centered around the message Asher lives by: which is to live life to the fullest and live out your purpose because you never know if today could be your last. As a brand new company, the two of them are continuing to work hard to spread their message, inspiring others to search for their purpose and become who they were made to be through their unique clothing line.

Toward the end of July, I began working on the backbone of an idea me and my friend Asher Schmitz created. I immediately turned to Webflow and taught myself how to build a website template from scratch. Many long, rigorous days and some 250+ hours later, the website was near completion.
